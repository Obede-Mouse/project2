    Intensive Care unit

    Our Intensive Care Unit stands out as one of the most advanced in the region. Our experienced team of intensivists provides medical care to all manner of critically ill patients.
  • Highly experienced intensivists available 24hrs
  • A compassionate and highly nursing team who are on hand 24hrs a day to support you and your patient
  • Patient to nurse ratio 1 to 1 or 2 to 1 where patients are very sick
  • Currently the only ICU in the country with a CRRT machine (dialysis for very critically ill Patients who cant tolerate regular dialysis)


    • conditions managed by the team:


    • Acute life threatening medical emergencies of any kind
    • Advanced cardiac (heart) support
    • Acute kidney dialysis (machines artificially remove body toxins)
    • Advanced respiratory support (ventilator breathes for the patient)
    • Advanced neuro support (for patients with brain injuries, stroke and epilepsy)
    • Paediatric intensive care
    • Newborn intensive care (caters to babies as premature as 24 weeks)
    • Post transplant care
    • Highly infectious disease care e.g Ebola,Marburg
    • End of life care for terminally ill patients
    • Advanced obstetric critical care (maternal critical illness)
